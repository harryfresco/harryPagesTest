the fake rolex.

from a car boot sale


Spotted at the last stall in a carboot sale, I picked this up and immediatly felt it was an automatic watch, but in the case of a replica Rolex.
Bringin it home, I opened it up and found the automatic winding gears weren't working, and neither was the balace wheel. I realised it was cheaper and easier to purchase a new, identical movement. I did bend the second hand in the process.
