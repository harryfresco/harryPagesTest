the mithras.

mithras

from an unknown market stall


From a very weird market stall vendor, i gave him a fiver for it. It wasn't working, but I thought I would put my watchmiking skills to good use.
After bringing it back home and opening the back, it only seemed that the balance wheel had been lodged by a metal shaving. Removing it brought it back to life. Easy.
The next problem was that the crystal was very scratched, but after a bit of polish, Polywatch, it was super shiny.

I tried to find this online, but couldn't get anywhere other than the fact that this Frecnh company, Mithras, were knocking around in the 1950s/1960s.
