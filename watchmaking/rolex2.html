a very fake rolex

from a weird lady in a market


Looking at the watch you can immediatley see that it's a fake, just from the word 'Quartz'. I picked it up and asked the lady how much it is, and she replied very quickly with "IT'S NOT WORKING IT'S NOT WORKING!"
I could see that, but thought I can fix it very easily by replacing the battery.
Getting home, I found a replacement battery but it still wouldn't work. So I bought an extremely cheap, matching quartz movement from eBay and I was a proud owner of a working Fauxlex.
