the stopwatch.

ww2 stopwatch

from a bomber pilot


From the same market stall as the Hefik, I asked if he had a stopwatch. (I just wanted one)
He said he only had one. His friend sitting next to him was also a ww2 nerd, and explained that this was used in a bomber plane, and the markings on the back meant it had been reissued to another pilot in 1941.
